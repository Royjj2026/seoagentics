How it works

Six layers. One
engine per client.

Most tools generate content. The Content Engine builds an understanding of each client first — then generates from that foundation, every time.

01
ICP & USP alignment
Brand strategy layer

Before writing a word, the engine understands who the client is selling to and what makes them the right choice. Every piece of content is angled to position the client as the solution for their ideal customer — not just topically relevant, but strategically aligned.

02
Style matching
Voice consistency layer

The engine is trained on each client's existing published content — their sentence structures, vocabulary, tone, and formatting preferences. New content slots seamlessly into their library. No tonal drift. No inconsistency. No editing to make it sound like them.

03
Intent & competitor analysis
SEO intelligence layer

Every brief is informed by what's actually ranking — not just keyword volume. The engine analyses search intent, reviews top-performing competitor content, and identifies the gaps and angles that give each piece a genuine ranking advantage before a single word is written.

04
Topic-modelled optimisation
Semantic SEO layer

Content is structured around topic modelling data — not just primary keywords. Each piece covers the semantic territory the algorithm expects, with entity relationships and supporting concepts built in. Content that ranks for the topic, not just the phrase.

05
Smart internal linking
Architecture layer

Internal and external links are recommended and added based on the client's content architecture — target pages, landing pages, pillar content, supporting articles, and internal linking rules. Every piece contributes to the site's authority structure, not just its own ranking.

06
Human authenticity layer
Detection-proof layer

The final pass. Every output runs through a refinement loop that calibrates sentence-level variation, structural unpredictability, and stylistic naturalness. The result isn't gamed to pass detection — it's written well enough that detection isn't a concern. Genuinely human-quality, every time.

Signature feature

The Human Authenticity Layer.

Every piece of content produced by the engine passes through a final refinement loop — engineered to produce output that reads as genuinely human-written, not AI-assisted.

This isn't a prompt trick. It's a structured process that runs style calibration, structural variation, and naturalness scoring before the content ever reaches your team.

1

Style calibration

Output is matched against the client's existing content at sentence and paragraph level — until variance is within human range.

2

Structural variation

Sentence rhythm, paragraph length, and transition patterns are adjusted to remove AI-predictable patterns.

3

Detection scoring

The output is scored against leading detection tools. If it doesn't pass, it loops until it does.

4

Final quality check

A logic and coherence pass ensures the authenticity refinements haven't compromised meaning, argument flow, or SEO structure.
